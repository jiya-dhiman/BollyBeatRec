How it works

From feeling to song, in three steps

Our AI reads the emotional landscape of your request, then finds and verifies the track that matches it.

i.

Semantic Intent Mapping

Adaptive Vibe Profiles

We analyze the underlying audio characteristics — energy level, danceability, and rhythm signature — to provide a full analytical profile of why a particular song fits your requested vibe.
