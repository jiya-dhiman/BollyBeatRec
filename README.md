# BollyBeat Lounge
An AI-powered discovery engine that translates your personal vibes, memories, and moods into custom Bollywood music soundtracks.

## How it Works
1. **Input:** You describe your current mood or a specific scenario (e.g., "A late-night drive during a monsoon").
2. **AI Engine:** The app processes your semantic description to find a matching track.
3. **Discover:** The dashboard presents the perfect song with metadata (vibe, tempo, energy) and a direct link to listen.
